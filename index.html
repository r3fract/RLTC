The Penalty Box Canteen

Unique arena food experience in Fort Frances

The Penalty Box Canteen is a community-rooted food concession inside the Memorial Sports Centre in Fort Frances, Ontario. Founded by Riley Pollard in 2024 with the mission of delivering a fast, fun, high-quality arena food experience that blends hockey culture, nostalgia, and local pride.

From classic arena favorites to creative comfort food combinations, we serve the Fort Frances hockey community and visitors with pride, bringing that authentic hockey canteen vibe that feels like home.
